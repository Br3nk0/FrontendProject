ENIAC THE BEGINING OF....

ENIAC,Electronic Numerical Integrator And Computer was the first electronic general-purpose computer. It was Turing-complete, digital, and capable of being reprogrammed to solve "a large class of numerical problems". When ENIAC was announced in 1946 it was heralded in the press as a "Giant Brain". But The ENIAC wasn't for personal use. The first Personal Computer, or PC was introduced in the seventies.
